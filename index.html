About me

My name is Elanchezhian, and I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering with aa strong focus on 3D modeling, animation and game development. I specialize in creating detailed 3D models and bringing them to life through animation using Blender. As a game developer, I leverage Unity and C# to transform creative concepts into games. With a solid foundation in computer science, I eagarly waiting for learning inovation and new technologies.

What I do?

3D Modelling

I create 3d models of characters, objects, environments or assets using specialized software like Blender. These models are the foundations of any 3D projects, whether it's for games, flims or simulations.

Animation

Animation involves bringing the 3D models into life through movement. I work with keyframes, rigging and physics to create fluid motion for charaters, objects or cameras in both pre-rendered content and games.

Game Development

As a game developer, I'm focused an programming and creating interactive experiences. This involves not just designing assets but also coding the logic, gameplay mechanics and systems that make the game functional. I likely use game engine like Unity for this.

VFX

As a beginner in visual effects, I focus on creating digital effects that would be difficult to achieve through practical methods using Blender.
